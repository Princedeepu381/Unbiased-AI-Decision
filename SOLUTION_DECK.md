## Slide 2: The Problem (The "Why")
- **The Black Box**: Machine Learning models are making life-altering decisions (hiring, loans) without transparent accountability.
- **Hidden Bias**: Historical data often contains systemic bias that AI amplifies.
- **Complexity Barrier**: Current fairness tools are built for researchers, not for policy makers or auditors.
- **The Cost**: Unfair AI leads to legal risks, brand damage, and social exclusion.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution (Aegis One)
- **Cinematic Auditing**: A high-performance dashboard that makes fairness data visual and intuitive.
- **Gemini Governance Trace**: Translates complex math (DIR, DPD, Equalized Odds) into plain-English AI insights.
- **One-Click Mitigation**: Automatically re-balances datasets using mathematical weighting to remove bias.
- **Audit-Ready Reports**: Instant PDF generation for compliance and governance records.

---

## Slide 4: Architecture (Powered by Google)
- **Compute**: Google Cloud Run (Serverless, Scalable, Secure).
- **Intelligence**: Google Gemini 1.5 Flash (Contextual bias explanation & mitigation strategy).
- **Frontend**: Next.js 14 + Recharts for real-time visualization.
- **Backend**: FastAPI + Fairlearn (State-of-the-art fairness algorithms).

---

## Slide 5: How it Works (The Logic)
1. **Ingest**: Auditor uploads a dataset.
2. **Analyze**: System identifies sensitive features and calculates disparity scores.
3. **Explain**: Gemini analyzes the scores and provides a human-readable verdict.
4. **Fix**: Auditor applies reweighing to equalize selection rates across groups.
5. **Verify**: System shows a Before vs. After comparison to prove impact.
